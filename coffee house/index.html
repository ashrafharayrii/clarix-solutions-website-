Our Story

Crafting Moments, One Cup at a Time

Born in the heart of Amman, Clarix Coffee House is a sanctuary for coffee lovers, sweet seekers, and juice enthusiasts. We source the finest beans from around the world and pair them with locally inspired desserts and hand-pressed juices.

Every corner of our space is designed to make you feel at home — whether you're here for a morning espresso, a midday meeting, or an evening treat.
