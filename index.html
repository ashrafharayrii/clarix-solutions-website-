About Us

The Team Behind Your Success

"We don't sell software. We solve business problems."

We are Clarix Solutions, a data and web development company based in Amman, Jordan. We specialize in building custom business dashboards and professional websites that help business owners understand their numbers, manage their operations, and make smarter decisions every day.

Our team combines expertise in data analytics, software development, and business operations. We believe that every business, no matter its size, deserves access to the same powerful tools that large companies use. That is why we build affordable, custom systems — not generic templates — so that you get exactly what your business needs to grow and stay ahead of the competition.
